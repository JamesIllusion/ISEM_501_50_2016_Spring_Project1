Summer in Chicago

Summer is always the most exciting time in Chicago. There are lots of events you can find when summer comes. You can run along the lake shore, play volleyball on the beach, enjoy the sunshine in parks, fishing in the boat. Everything you can imagine about summer, Chicago has it.

Every night of Wednesday and Saturday, there will be fireworks from the Navy Pier. Every August, there will be Air Water Show on the lake. Everyday, there will be free music concert in Millinem Park.

Please enjoy !
